# Progression And Equipment Detail

## Parent Authority

Global rules live in `../../content-systems-long-term-design.md`, especially the progression and equipment sections.

## Boundary

This detail area defines long-term growth and collection rules:

- hero level and rank;
- hero skill unlocks and mana/cooldown use;
- corps level and level-cap training;
- corps equipment level through city resources and workshop capacity;
- hero equipment slots, grades, and collection pool;
- token / command item identity.

## To Refine

- First-phase level caps.
- Rank unlock thresholds.
- Corps level experience sources.
- Corps equipment-level costs by class and fantasy form.
- Equipment grade effects and first sample equipment pool.

## Non-Goals

- Heavy random-affix equipment farming.
- Individual corps item equipment.
- Infinite level scaling that replaces city, command, or build decisions.
