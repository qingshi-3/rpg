# Acceptance

Accepted by user directive on 2026-07-07.

Accepted requirement:

- Battle preparation must not require choosing an objective deployment/target area.
- Clicking a battle group in the preparation roster enters formation-follow placement mode.
- Clicking a legal deployment position places the group.
- After placement, a non-blocking top-center prompt tells the player to right-click a destination.
- Right-clicking a valid reachable destination during preparation sets the selected group or selected groups' initial destination beacon.
- Runtime movement and presentation movement remain decoupled: beacon flow fields guide Runtime path decisions, while visual movement still follows Runtime movement events.
