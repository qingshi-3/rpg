# Product Index

This directory answers market and product questions: what the game is, who it is for, what tags it should use, and what promises it makes to players.

## Routes

- Product positioning: `positioning.md`

## Product Layer Rule

Product documents must describe the player-facing genre and promise. Use gameplay pillars as the source of truth for tags and store positioning. Worldbuilding expressions such as summoning, historical figures, and inter-world arrivals may support the pitch, but they must not replace the gameplay category.
