# Vision

## Game Type

- 2D top-down pixel art.
- Grid tactical.
- Turn-based.
- Light RPG plus strategy plus command system.

## Core Pitch

The player uses shared Action Points to coordinate heroes and troops on a battlefield where enemy intent is predictable.

The player solves tactical situations through spatial play, behavior rules, and tactical cards.

## Differentiation

- Heroes are directly controlled and deterministic.
- Troops are rule-driven and execute automatically.
- Enemy Intent is revealed in advance.
- Cards are tactical modifiers, not the main system.
- AP is the single resource that unifies all actions.

## Target Player Experience

- "I am solving the situation, not just comparing stats."
- "I can change the future through Intent."
- "I am commanding, not micromanaging every unit."
