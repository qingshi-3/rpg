# Vision

## Game Type

- 2D top-down pixel art.
- Fantasy world-journey RPG.
- Strategic world ecology.
- Enterable and operable WorldSite maps.
- Turn-based grid tactics.

## Core Pitch

The player travels through a fantasy world as a character or small party, not as a default faction ruler.

The world runs on a Sanguo Qunying-style strategic ecology: factions, WorldSites, armies, threats, and opportunities move and collide on the big map. The player enters that living situation, chooses how to intervene, resolves local conflict through site operation, negotiation, or tactical battle, and the result writes back into the world.

## Product Positioning

The project is not a pure strategic conquest game.

The project is not a menu-only RPG where sites are only shop, quest, or management panels.

The intended product shape is:

```text
Fantasy world-journey RPG
with a strategic NPC/faction ecology underneath
and persistent enterable WorldSites as the main interaction space.
```

Later progression may allow the player to establish a faction, occupy WorldSites, and command larger forces, but that is an advanced path rather than the default starting identity.

## Differentiation

- The big map is an active world surface, not a list of level nodes.
- NPC factions and armies can move, attack, defend, ally, and create opportunities without waiting for the player.
- A `WorldSite` is a persistent small map with peacetime operation, wartime battle, facility state, NPC/object memory, and result writeback.
- Wilderness between WorldSites can host encounters, interceptions, rescues, resource opportunities, and faction conflicts.
- Turn-based battle is one resolution mode inside the world, not an isolated combat mode.
- Player growth should change world intervention options, not only combat numbers.

## Target Player Experience

- "I am traveling through a world that keeps moving."
- "I can enter a local situation and decide what I want from it."
- "This place remembers what happened here."
- "My battle choices change the world map, and world preparation changes the battle."
- "I am not only clearing fights; I am changing my position in a living world."
