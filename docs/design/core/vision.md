# Vision

## Game Type

- 2D top-down pixel art.
- Summoned-character relationship-driven strategic RPG.
- Strategic world ecology.
- Enterable and operable WorldSite maps.
- Turn-based grid tactics.

## Core Pitch

Project title:

```text
异世界的召唤
```

The game is about an original fantasy world disrupted by a summoning mechanism that brings people from other eras, legends, and authored worlds into the same living world.

Summoned characters carry old relationships, values, grudges, obligations, and social positions into the new world. The player does not manage every person or event directly. The player makes limited, high-impact interventions: who to summon, who to trust, who to promote, where to personally intervene, and which world crisis to let subordinates or local forces resolve.

## Product Positioning

The project is not a pure strategic conquest game.

The project is not a linear chapter-based story RPG.

The project is not a menu-only RPG where sites are only shop, quest, or management panels.

The intended product shape is:

```text
召唤群像关系驱动的战略 RPG
with a living strategic world underneath
and tactical battles as high-expression local conflict resolution.
```

The world should be authored as a large initial situation: factions, species conflicts, historical grudges, summoned people, WorldSites, armies, opportunities, and major conditional events. After that, systems advance the world and the player chooses where to intervene.

## Differentiation

- Summoned origin is flavor and content context; gameplay runs on abstract relationships, social attributes, rank, and control authority.
- Character content should become world pressure, loyalty risk, event hooks, and battle stakes, not only collection.
- The big map is an active world surface, not a list of level nodes.
- NPC factions, armies, and summoned actors can move, attack, defend, ally, betray, and create opportunities without waiting for the player.
- A `WorldSite` is a persistent small map with peacetime operation, wartime battle, facility state, NPC/object memory, and result writeback.
- Wilderness between WorldSites can host encounters, interceptions, rescues, resource opportunities, and faction conflicts.
- Turn-based battle is a high-expression resolution mode inside the world, not an isolated combat mode.
- Player growth should change world intervention options, appointment authority, summoning reach, and tactical control authority, not only combat numbers.

## Target Player Experience

- "The world keeps moving even when I do not personally handle every crisis."
- "These summoned people brought old bonds into a new world, and my choices are changing those bonds."
- "I cannot optimize everything, so choosing who and where to intervene matters."
- "This place remembers what happened here."
- "My battle choices change the world map, and world preparation changes the battle."
- "I am not only clearing fights; I am deciding which people, relationships, and crises deserve direct intervention."
